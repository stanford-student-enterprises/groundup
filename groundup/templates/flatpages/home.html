GROUND UP

Ground Up is a wholesale partner of Blue Bottle Coffee Co., one of the Bay Area’s foremost artisan coffee roasters. Started as a joint business venture between AOL and Stanford Student Enterprises (SSE), Ground Up’s aim is to service the Peninsula with diligently prepared organic coffee and espresso drinks. All of Ground Up’s baristas are Blue Bottle-trained, resulting in coffee drinks that are made with keen attention to detail and the finest organic ingredients.
